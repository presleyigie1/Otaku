Naruto lores

It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. The story is told in two parts – the first set in Naruto's pre-teen years, and the second in his teens.In the first part of naruto he struggled with depression as he was ostracized by the people of the leaf village as they viewed him as a monster rather than a child that need affection. They viewed him as a monster because when he was just a baby his father and mother sealed half a tailed beast into his body for the sake of the village but the people of the village do not know of this but only that he is the holder of the nine tails tailed beast. The other half was sealed within his father with the help of his mother but sadly they both died in the choas of the sealing jutsu which left him alone in the world as an orphan but while his father was dying he asked his mentor the third hokage to take care of his son for him and not to let naruto know about how they had died.

Hokages

  • 1st Hokage Hashirama Senju also known as the God of Shinobi, the very first Hokage and one of the founders of the leaf village.

  • 2nd Hokage Tobirama Senju, the younger bother of Hashirama and the inventor of many jutsu such as Flying thunder god technique and the Shadow clone jutsu.

  • 3rd Hokage Hiruzen Sarutobi, the number one student of the second hokage and was known as the professor as he managed to master mutiple high ranking jutsu and was also the teacher of the legendary sannin of the leaf.

  • 4th Hokage Minato Namikaze was also known as the yellow flash of the leaf, one of the fasts ninja in Konoha and the other 5 villages. He was also the create of the Rasengan a high SS ranking jutsu.

  • 5th Hokage Tsunade Senju also know as the granddaughter of the 1st Hokage and one of the 3 legendary sannin. She is famed as the world's strongest kunoichi and its greatest medical-nin.

  • 6th Hokage Kakashi Hatake, a student of the 4th hokage and also known as the copy-cat ninja for his amazing ability to copy any move he has seen once.

  • 7th Hokage Naruto Uzumaki, one of the two strongest ninja in the world of shinobi also known as the hero of the leaf. Stundet to the 6th Hokage and one of the 3 legendary sannin jiraiya.
